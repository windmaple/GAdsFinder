This simple tool aims to help Sellside TAMs surface sporadic Google ads.

A typical use case is to find an ad from a syndicator partner who only owns a small share of the publisher's traffic. The extension will keep refreshing the webpage until it sees the desirable Google ad.

Usage: use 'ca-pub-XXX' as the search string, where ca-pub-XXX is the pub ID
