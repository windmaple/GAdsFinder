This simple tool aims to help surface surface sporadic Google Adx ads.

A typical use case is to find an ad from a syndicator partner (NPM) who only owns a small share of the publisher's traffic.
The extension will keep refreshing the webpage until it sees the specified search strings in the responses, which means it has found a desirable Google ad.

Usage: use 'ca-pub-XXX' as the search string, where ca-pub-XXX is the publisher ID. Ad tag ID and ad size (e.g., 300x250) can also be used.
